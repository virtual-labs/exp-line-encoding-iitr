The following instructions are for Differential Manchester Encoding-
1) Enter the data bits you wish to encode.
2) Set the voltage of Data signals.
3) Click on Submit button.
4) Your Output digital signal will be plotted live for you.
5) Try out the other encoding schemes just by clicking on dropdown visible at the top stating Choose a Scheme.

Rules for the encoding:
It combines the idea of RZ and NRZ-I. If the next bit is 0,transition will occur but if the next bit is 1, no transition will occur.
